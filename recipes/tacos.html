Tacos

Description

One of mankind's greatest creations since the inception of civilization itself. It doesn't need to be complicated to be amazing, simply a protein contained within a shell accompanied by some tasty toppings.

Ingredients

  • Soft Tortilla
  • Ground beef
  • Curry powder
  • Shredded cheese
  • Salsa
  • Lettuce
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef
  2. Add the curry powder to the beef
  3. Microwave tortilla between 2 moist paper towels for 15 seconds
  4. Place meat on center of tortilla
  5. Add toppings
  6. Fold tortilla and consume
  7. Enjoy!
